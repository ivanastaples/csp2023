surf club

Surfing was something I started doing with friends during Quarantine. I never thought I'd end up becoming the president at RB's Surf Club! I helped raise over $1,900 to form a school competition team. We competed in the Scholastic Surf Series Tournament. I even won a few medals for placing which is something I never thought I'd be able to do. Surfing is my favorite hobby, and I will continue to surf in college.

It is a big hobby and commitment of mine that I put a lot of time and effort into.


peer counseling

It truly is amazing to be apart of such an organized club that makes such a difference on campus!
